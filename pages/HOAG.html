HOAG

Junior Auxiliary Volunteer (2016-2018)

I served at the HOAG hospital located in Irvine. My main position was at the front desk located at the main entrance. I usually helped a staff member who would already be stationed there with any tasks that they needed help with. In addition, at times I would be alone at the desk and would be the first person anyone would meet if they used the main entrance to the hospital. My duties at this position ranged from prepping the required forms new visitors would have to sign to directing individuals either to the specific doctor they wished to visit or to escort them to the emergency room.
