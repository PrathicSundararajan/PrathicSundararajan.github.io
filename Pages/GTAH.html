Suite Attendant (2019)

During the football season, I worked in the suites section of the stadium. I was in charge of prepping the suites before the game, ensuring that suite holders were satisfied with their experience during the game and then cleaning the suites after the game. I was essentially the first person the suite holders would go to if they needed anything at any point during their experience.
